Overview

Your life artifacts.
Shelved beautifully.

Shelf is a remarkably simple tool.
It’s a place for keeping things worth remembering and revisiting. From recipes that remind you of home to notes on happiness, love, or personal obsessions — Shelf displays your work with beauty and care.

Its creative simplicity makes it a unique medium for self-expression — and an elegant way to keep interesting ideas and parts of you worth remembering. Easy to organize and easy to come back to. That’s Shelf.

Artifacts

Fill them with your life.

Timeless forms. Notes with scribbles, a 1984 Macintosh, and radios from Braun. Every item in Shelf takes after some beloved artifact of our pyramiding achievements.

Stunning technicolor. Shelf’s use of color is meaningful. It helps group items into spaces, and serves as a general background for engaging with an item’s content.

Time Capsule

Talk to your future.

Time travel. Really. Time capsules can hold any item in Shelf — even rooms. Use them to send bits of you to the future, giving yourself a perspective over how you change over time.

Shelf doesn’t support passwords. It supports introspection. Time capsules are secure and can’t be opened once sealed, honoring a tradition that dates back hundreds of years.
